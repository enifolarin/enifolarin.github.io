Most entrepreneurs, copywriters miss one very important thing in their marketing.

And that probably may be the reason they’re not converting enough or even worse, at all.

And that one thing is market research.

To sell anything to anyone you have to know who they are. Why?

Oh common, because they are your prospects. And you want them to know that you understand them, what they want, how they feel and that most importantly, you can help them.
